Description:


     Fixed Maturity Policies (FMPs) are a type of investment policy offered by insurance companies that provide a fixed return to the policyholder at the end of a specified investment period. FMPs are similar to fixed deposits in banks, but they are offered by insurance companies and are subject to different rules and regulations.

FMPs invest in debt instruments such as bonds, debentures, and money market instruments, with a fixed maturity date and a predetermined interest rate. The investment period for FMPs typically ranges from one month to five years.
